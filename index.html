About Me

Hi there! I'm Cinthia Salina, though most people know me as Sterki Herz. I'm a digital artist based in Buenos Aires, Argentina.

Creating characters and stories is one of my favorite things, and I’ve always had a soft spot for Medieval Fantasy and RPGs!

Right now, I’m a college student, but I also take on freelance gigs when I can—and sometimes dive into fun side projects and Game Jams too!
